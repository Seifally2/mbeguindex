Growing the Future of Agriculture

For over 20 years, GreenHarvest has been at the forefront of sustainable farming and agricultural innovation.

Our Story

Mbegu Nzuri Biotech Farms Ltd. was established to address the challenges faced by farmers in accessing quality seeds and seedlings. Leveraging biotechnology, we specialize in producing certified seeds and seedlings that meet international quality standards.

Our Vision

To transform agriculture through innovative seed technologies for sustainable food production.

Our Mission

To empower farmers by providing superior, science-backed agricultural solutions.

5+ Years of Experience

Trusted by farmers and consumers alike
